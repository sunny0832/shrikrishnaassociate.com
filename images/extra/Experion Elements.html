About M3M Altitude

M3M Altitude is a newly launched ultra-luxury residential project located in Sector 65, Gurgaon, on the prestigious Golf Course Road Extension. Spread across 5 acres of lush green landscape, the development features 3 high-rise towers offering meticulously crafted 4 BHK residences and penthouses.

This RERA-approved project (RERA No. 48 of 2024) brings together world-class specifications with only 4 units per core, ensuring the highest level of privacy and exclusivity. Apartment sizes range from 3755 to 4270 sq ft, with spacious layouts and premium interiors that redefine luxury living.

Designed by the globally acclaimed UHA London, M3M Altitude offers a range of lifestyle amenities that bring modern comfort and elegance to your doorstep. From thoughtfully planned living spaces to high-end facilities, this project embodies sophistication and excellence in every detail.

Whether you're looking for your next home or a premium investment opportunity in Gurgaon, M3M Altitude stands as a benchmark in luxury real estate on Golf Course Road Extension.
